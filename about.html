About Mock USPTO

A multi-page patent review suite built to look polished and actually work locally.

This merged version combines the strong visual identity from the screenshots with the working claim-chart and office-action features from the later builds.

What is included

  • A polished examination console with weighted review metrics.
  • A dedicated claim-chart page with limitation mapping.
  • A dedicated office-action page with memo generation.
  • A searchable 20-case patent law database.
  • Local browser persistence and export tools.

What it is not

  • Not a hosted production law-firm deployment.
  • Not a substitute for confidential document management, attorney judgment, or live prior-art search.
  • Not a final legal opinion engine.

It is a strong local platform and portfolio-ready legal-tech system you can run, show, and keep extending.
